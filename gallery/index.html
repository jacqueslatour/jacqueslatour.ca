What’s in the bundle

Everything a recipient needs to verify — nothing they don’t

Each Download bundle is a small .zip containing three files. No account, no portal, no call-back to us required to trust it.

  • The document

    The signed .html file itself — open it in any browser.

  • The .vrfy sidecar

    The cryptographic manifest: a SHA-256 hash of the document plus Ed25519 and ML-DSA-44 post-quantum signatures over it.

  • HOW-TO-VERIFY.txt

    Extract the folder and check the document with TrustDID Verify. That’s it.

A signature proves two things: who issued the document (a public did:web identity anchored in DNS) and that it hasn’t changed since. Alter one character and verification fails — try it on the tampered demo.
