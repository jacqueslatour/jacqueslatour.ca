Clearwater Advisory Group Announces
Strategic Partnership with Nordic Trust Alliance

✓ Signed

OTTAWA, ON — March 1, 2026

Clearwater Advisory Group, a leading provider of compliance and risk management services, today announced a strategic partnership with Nordic Trust Alliance (NTA), a Scandinavian consortium specializing in digital identity infrastructure for regulated industries.

The partnership will combine Clearwater's expertise in North American regulatory frameworks with NTA's advanced cryptographic identity solutions to deliver end-to-end document trust services for enterprises operating across multiple jurisdictions.

Key Highlights

  • Joint development of cross-border document verification standards using decentralized identifiers (DIDs)
  • Integration of post-quantum cryptographic signatures (ML-DSA-44) into all shared service offerings
  • Establishment of a bilateral trust registry enabling mutual recognition of signed documents
  • Pilot program with six financial institutions across Canada, Norway, and Sweden

"Document integrity is no longer optional in regulated industries," said Elena Vasquez, CEO of Clearwater Advisory Group. "By partnering with Nordic Trust Alliance, we can offer our clients cryptographic proof of document authenticity that works across borders and withstands both current and future threats."

Marcus Lindqvist, Director of NTA, added: "The combination of DNSSEC-anchored identities with post-quantum signatures creates an assurance level that no other document signing platform can match. This partnership brings that capability to enterprises on both sides of the Atlantic."

About Clearwater Advisory Group

Clearwater Advisory Group is a boutique consulting firm specializing in compliance, risk management, and digital trust solutions for regulated industries. Founded in 2018, the firm serves financial services, healthcare, and government clients across North America.

About Nordic Trust Alliance

Nordic Trust Alliance is a consortium of Scandinavian technology firms focused on building decentralized identity infrastructure for enterprise use. NTA members serve clients in banking, insurance, and public sector across the Nordic region.
